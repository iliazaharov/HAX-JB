JetBrains develops tools for technology creators, and at the Human-AI Experience Research team, we’re all about making those tools even better. Our team specializes in user studies and prototyping to invent and investigate human-AI interaction scenarios that enhance the work of technology creators.

Our Research

Our research spans three core areas: Design, Impact, and Quality of Interaction with AI. We investigate critical questions, such as the most effective HAX implementation in an IDE and development lifecycle, the influence of in-IDE HAX on users' experiences and characteristics, and the key attributes of AI output that better support users. By understanding how developers think, learn, and work with AI, we can suggest new seamless and intuitive AI feature ideas and prototypes tailored for JetBrains users' needs.

Our Approach

Our approach goes beyond traditional user experience research and A/B testing. We leverage our expertise in cognitive psychology and data analysis to conduct experimental mixed-method studies, adhering to rigorous academic standards. This expertise also guides us in addressing technical challenges with attention to unique human characteristics, ultimately optimizing the user interface and functionality of AI-driven JetBrains tools.

What Do We Bring to the Table

  • Innovation Ahead of Product Roadmaps: We generate knowledge and novel ideas for features and forms of HAX, staying ahead of the curve and ensuring JetBrains remains a leader in AI-driven development tools.
  • Creating and Enhancing AI-Feature Prototypes: We leverage our research for developing new AI-feature prototypes for JetBrains tools and for increasing user awareness of tools developed by other teams, enhancing user flows in IDEs and overall development workflows both within and outside the IDE.
